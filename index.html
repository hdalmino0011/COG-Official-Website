Who We Are

About Us

Welcome to The Church of God (Truth, Justice, and Righteousness) — a community of believers dedicated to walking in the fullness of God's Word. We are a Bible-based, Spirit-led congregation committed to proclaiming the Gospel of Jesus Christ with clarity, compassion, and conviction.

Our Mission

To proclaim Truth, practice Justice, and live Righteousness — reflecting the character of God in every area of life and ministry.

Our Foundation

We are grounded in the Holy Scriptures — the inspired, infallible Word of God — which serves as our ultimate authority for faith and conduct.

Our Community

We are a family of believers from all walks of life, united in love and purpose — worshipping together, growing together, and serving together.

Our Vision

To be a pillar and ground of the truth — equipping believers and reaching the lost with the life-changing message of Jesus Christ.

What We Stand For

Our Beliefs

01

The Holy Trinity

We believe in one God eternally existing in three Persons — the Father, the Son (Jesus Christ), and the Holy Spirit — co-equal and co-eternal.

02

Authority of Scripture

We believe the Holy Bible is the inspired, inerrant Word of God — the supreme authority for all matters of faith, doctrine, and Christian living.

03

Salvation Through Christ

We believe that salvation comes through faith in Jesus Christ alone — His death, burial, and resurrection — and is received through repentance and faith.

04

The Gospel Message

We believe in the Great Commission — the mandate to preach the Gospel of Jesus Christ to every nation, tongue, and people as witnesses of God's saving grace.

05

The Holy Spirit

We believe in the person and work of the Holy Spirit — who convicts, regenerates, indwells, and empowers believers for holy living and effective service.

06

The Second Coming

We believe in the literal, personal, and imminent return of Jesus Christ — the blessed hope of every believer — to establish His eternal kingdom.
